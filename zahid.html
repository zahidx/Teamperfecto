Literature Review

Zahidul Islam

IOT BASED INDUSTRIAL MONITORING SYSTEM

IoT based Humidity and Temperature Monitoring Systemusing Arduino Uno

Summary

The research paper titled "IoT based Industrial Monitoring System" presents a system that integrates big data and intelligent sensors to monitor the safety and efficiency of manufacturing industries. The proposed technology uses the Blynk app, which allows monitoring of the system's status from anywhere globally. The system uses Arduino to control the sensors, and ESP8266 chips connect the system to a Wi-Fi network to deliver data to the Blynk app. The system uses smoke, temperature, and humidity sensors to monitor the machine surroundings. The results demonstrate that adding automation technology to any manufacturing industry can assure safety and well-being, prevent accidents and reduce chances of loss in the machinery world. The paper emphasizes the importance of technology advancement in modernizing and automating industries. The study concludes that IoT monitoring bridges the gap between devices and businesses by collecting and analyzing a wide range of IoT data at a web scale across connected devices, consumers, and apps.

Summary

The research paper presents an Internet of Things (IoT) based humidity and temperature monitoring system that utilizes Arduino Uno and ESP8266 Wi-Fi module. The system collects data from sensors measuring temperature and humidity in the surroundings and stores it on the cloud for further analysis and control of electronic devices. The system is designed to cut down on human involvement and overall expenditure while enhancing the efficiency of the mechanism. The paper discusses the importance of IoT in our daily lives and its potential for growth and development. The system presented in the paper can be used for monitoring temperature and humidity in various industries like pharmaceuticals and controlling electrical or electronic devices present in homes and industries. The experimental results demonstrate that the system can monitor the live temperature and humidity of the surroundings and the soil moisture of any plant using Arduino UNO. Overall, the paper provides an interesting and secure approach to humidity and temperature monitoring using IoT.

IoT Based Humidity, Temperature, and Gas Monitoring Using Arduino UNO

IoT Based Industrial Security Control and Monitoring System Using NodeMCU

Summary

The research paper discusses the use of IoT in monitoring the temperature, humidity, and gas in the environment using Arduino UNO and ESP8266-01 wifi module. The collected data is stored in the Thingspeak cloud platform, and changes in the environment are updated as a database through cloud computing. The paper highlights the importance of monitoring environmental conditions to prevent accidents, and IoT technology provides an efficient solution. The study demonstrates the effectiveness of the proposed system in monitoring environmental conditions and shows that IoT technology has provided many opportunities in different domains. Other related research papers are also discussed, including an automatic weather monitoring system, a greenhouse monitoring and controlling system, and an IoT-based temperature and humidity detecting device for monitoring agricultural parameters.

Summary

The research paper proposes an IoT based intelligent security system for industries that uses wireless sensors/actuators and the NodeMCU microcontroller. The system can detect environmental deviations such as chemical overflow, gas leakage, fire/boiler explosion, and short circuit, as well as measure temperature, humidity, and undesired sound using various smart sensors. The data is sent to the proprietor via email and SMS through the internet. The system can be controlled and monitored from anywhere and anytime through a computer, mobile or any smart device. The paper discusses the advantages of this system and provides implementation details on how the electrical appliances are controlled and monitored using a web-based application. The proposed system offers a portable device and a combination of embedded electronics and computer programming. The research work offers significant contributions to the field of IoT-based security systems.

Internet of Things (IOT) Based Gas Leakage Monitoring and Alerting System with MQ-2 Sensor

Summary

The research paper titled "Internet of Things (IoT) Based Gas Leakage Monitoring and Alerting System with MQ-2 Sensor" describes the design and implementation of a microcontroller-based gas detection and alerting system. The system is designed to detect hazardous gases like LPG and propane using an MQ-2 sensor and display the gas level on an LCD screen. If the gas level exceeds a certain limit, an alarm is triggered, and an alert message is sent to an authorized person via email through the internet. The system is built using an ARM Cortex-M4 development board and wireless network connectivity. The paper also provides a brief introduction to IoT and its potential impact on various industries and sectors. The literature review includes related research work on intelligent security systems, wireless monitoring systems, and gas sensors for vehicle cabin air quality monitoring. Finally, the paper describes the material properties and design specifications of the ARM Cortex-M4 development board used in the gas leakage monitoring system.
