Components and Budget

NodeMCU ESP8266

NodeMCU is an open-source firmware and development kit for building Internet of Things (IoT) applications. It is based on the ESP8266 Wi-Fi module and comes with a Lua scripting language, which allows developers to write code for connecting the device to the internet and interacting with various sensors and actuators. It is a small, low-cost device with built-in support for Wi-Fi connectivity and several GPIO pins.

Price : 400 Taka

Gas Sensor

The MQ-3 gas sensor is a semiconductor device used to detect the presence of alcohol vapors in the air. It operates on the principle of gas conductivity and is sensitive to ethanol, as well as other gases such as benzene, carbon monoxide, and propane. The sensor outputs an analog voltage that increases as the concentration of alcohol vapors in the air increases. It is commonly used in breathalyzers, gas leak detectors, and other alcohol detection applications.

Price : 170 Taka

Bluetooth Module

The Bluetooth HC-05 module is a wireless communication module that uses Bluetooth technology to enable communication between electronic devices. It is commonly used in microcontroller-based projects to wirelessly transmit data between devices. The module operates in the 2.4GHz frequency band and supports a range of up to 10 meters. It is easy to use and can be configured using simple AT commands. It is commonly used in robotics, home automation, and other IoT projects.

Price : 340 Taka

Temparature and Humidity sensor

A temperature and humidity sensor is a device that measures the temperature and relative humidity of the surrounding air. It consists of a small integrated circuit that contains a temperature sensor and a humidity sensor and is commonly used in weather monitoring, indoor climate control, and other applications where temperature and humidity are important factors.

Price : 174 Taka

I2C LCD Display (16x2)

An I2C LCD display (16x2) is a display that can show up to 16 characters per line and up to 2 lines of text. It uses a simple 2-wire communication protocol called I2C to communicate with other devices. It has an integrated backlight and contrast adjustment and is commonly used to display messages and data in different projects.

Price : 360 Taka

Connecting Wires

A jumper wire connects two points in an electronic circuit using a thin insulated wire with male connectors on each end. It is commonly used in prototyping and breadboarding to create temporary connections between different components of an electronic circuit.

Price : 160 Taka
